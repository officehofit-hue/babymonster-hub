About BABYMONSTER

BABYMONSTER is a seven-member South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment. The group debuted on November 27, 2023, with their single album BABYMONS7ER. The members are Ruka, Pharita, Asa, Haram, Rora, Chiquita, and Ahyeon.

Known for their powerful performances, exceptional vocal and rap skills, and captivating stage presence, BABYMONSTER has quickly risen to become one of K-pop's most exciting acts. Their music blends hip-hop, pop, and EDM influences with dynamic choreography.
